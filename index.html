Hey there, I'm Jessica! I'm a recent grad of The University of Texas at Austin where I studied Computer Engineering, and I'm currently an Engineering Resident at Google in Seattle, WA. My interests lie in the use of technology to impact the education and health industries. I'm particularly interested in the way nutrition and lifestyle affect a person's health and how empowering people with knowledge about their bodies through technology can impact their wellbeing and help them take action against preventative illnesses. I'm also passionate about blogging, teaching others about coding, supporting diversity efforts, and exploring the arts to improve myself as an engineer.
